# Chart Selection Justification

## 1. Sales Trend

### Business Question

How are sales changing over time?

### Chart Type

Line Chart

### Why This Chart

A line chart clearly illustrates trends and seasonality.

### Encodings

- X-axis: Order Date
- Y-axis: Sales
- Filter: Region, Category

### Design Principle

Time series should be displayed continuously.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided pie charts for temporal data.

---

## 2. Regional Performance

### Business Question

Which regions contribute the most sales and profit?

### Chart Type

Horizontal Bar Chart

### Encodings

- Region
- Sales
- Profit (Color)

### Design Principle

Easy comparison between categories.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided unnecessary 3D maps.

---

## 3. Category Profitability

### Business Question

Which product categories generate the highest profits?

### Chart Type

Horizontal Bar Chart

### Encodings

- Category
- Profit
- Sales (Label)

### Design Principle

Sorted bars improve readability.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided stacked bars that hide comparisons.

---

## 4. Customer Segment Analysis

### Business Question

Which customer segment performs best?

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Encodings

- Segment
- Sales
- Profit

### Design Principle

Simple comparison across groups.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided radar charts.

---

## 5. Shipping Performance

### Business Question

Which shipping mode experiences longer delivery times?

### Chart Type

Bar Chart

### Encodings

- Ship Mode
- Average Delivery Days

### Design Principle

Direct comparison between shipping methods.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided complex network diagrams.

---

## 6. Discount vs Profit

### Business Question

How do discounts influence profitability?

### Chart Type

Scatter Plot

### Encodings

- Discount
- Profit
- Category (Color)

### Design Principle

Scatter plots effectively reveal relationships.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided line charts for non-time relationships.

---

## 7. Return Analysis

### Business Question

Where do product returns occur most frequently?

### Chart Type

Highlight Table

### Encodings

- Category
- Segment
- Return Rate

### Design Principle

Color intensity highlights problem areas.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided pie charts for multiple-category comparison.
